ABOUT PROJECT:

Our project focuses on developing cutting-edge AI models specifically designed for voltage and current monitoring in Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs). We recognize that robust power management is paramount for the continuous and safe operation of AMRs in diverse environments. Traditional monitoring systems often react to failures; however, our AI-driven approach leverages machine learning to predict potential power anomalies before they occur. By analyzing real-time data from AMR power systems, our models can identify subtle deviations and patterns indicative of impending issues like battery degradation, fluctuating power draw, or component stress. This proactive capability allows for timely maintenance, prevents unexpected downtime, and significantly enhances the overall reliability and lifespan of autonomous fleets. Our goal is to ensure seamless, uninterrupted performance for your robots, driving efficiency and reducing operational costs.
